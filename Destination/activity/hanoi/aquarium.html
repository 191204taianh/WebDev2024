Lotte World Aquarium
With a total area of ​​9,094m², containing 67 large display tanks, Lotte World Aquarium Hanoi is currently home to more than 31,000 marine creatures of more than 400 species, simulating the marine world most closely. Endlessly inspired by the magic of the deep blue ocean and the typical cultural features of Vietnam, the Aquarium is designed with the imprint of Vietnamese folklore, based on the mythical stories of Ca Ong - the god who protects the fishermen in each voyage. Here, the image of Ca Ong is a symbol leading the journey of cruising the sea with many interesting things, helping visitors understand more about the traditional values ​​of Vietnam's seas.
