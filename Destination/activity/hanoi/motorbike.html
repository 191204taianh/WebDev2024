Hanoi Sightseeing on Motorbike - Half-Day Tour
Embark on a 4-hour guided cycling tour through Hanoi, exploring its iconic landmarks and vibrant culture. Visit Hoan Kiem Lake to uncover its history, head to St. Joseph's Cathedral for a glimpse of French colonial architecture, and experience the bustling Old Quarter. The tour also includes a visit to the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum for insights into Vietnam's history, followed by stops at the tranquil West Lake and the ancient Tran Quoc Pagoda. The adventure concludes at the peaceful Truc Bach Lake, offering a perfect blend of cultural exploration and scenic beauty.
