Suoi Tien Theme Park
The park is closely associated with Buddhism and so are its designs, featuring a number of Buddhist images, sculptures, and temples. While a regular theme park would have done with just a combination of amusement park elements, it is a unique characteristic in Suoi Tien that it combines amusement park elements with cultural and religious themes, making it an accessible destination for the parents and the children alike.
