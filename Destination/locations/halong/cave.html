Sung Sot Cave
During your travel journey to Ha Long, visiting Sung Sot Cave (Surprise Cave Vietnam) should be one of the top activities on your bucket list. Situated in a limestone mountain with an extremely developed karst terrain, the cave features two big chambers with a wealth of stalagmites of various shapes.
