Quang Ninh Museum & Library
Ha Long City in Quang Ninh Province is among the top tourist destinations in Vietnam. Notably, Quang Ninh Museum, the spectacular architectural work that faces the magnificent Ha Long Bay, is one of the landmarks of this land. It is a must-see museum in Quang Ninh that travelers should not miss out on.
