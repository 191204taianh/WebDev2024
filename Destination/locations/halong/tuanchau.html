Tuan Chau Island
Tuan Chau Island has strongly impressed both domestic and foreign tourists with an untouched beauty of soft white sand and crystal-clear blue waters as well as a wide selection of top-notch amenities and exhilarating entertainment activities. Therefore, it has become an unmissable destination when visiting the world-famous Ha Long Bay.
