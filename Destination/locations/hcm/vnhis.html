Museum of Vietnamese History
Museum of Vietnamese History first opened its doors in 1929. The Museum houses an unique collection of interesting artefacts for introducing Vietnam's history, from ancient times to more modern days. A statue of the Buddha with 1,000 eyes and 1,000 arms is also part of their collections.
