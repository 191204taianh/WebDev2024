Ba Ho Waterfall
This Nha Trang waterfall got its name from its unique structure as “Ba Ho” is literally translated into “three pools”. The waterfalls tumble down the mountain, through the primeval forest ecosystem, to form a structure of three cascades, adding a hallucinatory effect into the scenery. All three lakes have their own charms; however, if you want to explore the second and third pools’ majestic beauty, consider that the rocky trails can be challenging. So, the majority of tourists would choose the first tier serenity-pool to cool down the heat from the sun.
