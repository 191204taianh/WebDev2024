Embark on an unforgettable 2-day, 1-night adventure in Hanoi, where vibrant culture, rich history, and tantalizing cuisine await.

Arrive in the bustling capital and explore the iconic Hoan Kiem Lake and the historic Old Quarter. Wander through its lively streets, sampling delicious street food and visiting local artisans. In the afternoon, delve into Hanoi’s history at the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and the One Pillar Pagoda. Enjoy a traditional Vietnamese dinner before watching a captivating water puppet show, a unique cultural experience.

On the second day, after breakfast, visit the Temple of Literature, Vietnam’s first university, and stroll through its beautiful gardens. In the afternoon, enjoy a guided excursion to the serene West Lake, where you can relax at a lakeside café. Before departing, savor a final taste of Hanoi with a delicious lunch featuring local specialties. This immersive tour offers a perfect blend of exploration, relaxation, and culinary delights, leaving you with unforgettable memories of Vietnam’s capital.
