Experience the vibrant heart of Vietnam with our immersive 3-day, 2-night Hanoi tour, designed to showcase the city’s rich history, stunning architecture, and delectable cuisine.

Arrive in Hanoi and kick off your adventure with a visit to the iconic Hoan Kiem Lake, where you can enjoy a leisurely stroll and take in the serene beauty of the area. Explore the bustling streets of the Old Quarter, sampling local street food and discovering hidden gems. In the evening, indulge in a traditional Vietnamese dinner and experience a captivating water puppet show, a unique cultural performance steeped in history.

On day 2, after breakfast, embark on a full-day exploration of Hanoi's historical sites, starting with the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and the One Pillar Pagoda. Continue to the Temple of Literature, Vietnam’s first university, and soak in the peaceful atmosphere of its lush gardens. In the afternoon, visit the Vietnam Museum of Ethnology to learn about the country’s diverse cultures. Wrap up your day with a dinner at a local restaurant, followed by a relaxing evening at West Lake.

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before visiting the Tran Quoc Pagoda, the oldest Buddhist temple in Hanoi. Explore local markets for unique souvenirs and enjoy a last taste of Hanoi’s culinary delights at a popular street food spot. Depart with unforgettable memories and a deeper appreciation for this captivating city.

This comprehensive tour offers the perfect blend of exploration, cultural immersion, and culinary experiences, ensuring you leave with lasting memories of Hanoi.
